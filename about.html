What is tiwonk ?
'tiwonk' is a platfrom to bring back reading in the life of the generation. While self-help books have a lot of stuff (information + story of the author + the same information written over and over). 'tiwonk' only pick out the information and present it in a new and attractive way. It is like social media scrolling. Within few minutes a whole chapter of a book will be over without missing any important details.
How it helps you ?
Reading a book everyday is important to grow in life. But in this rapid fast world - students, hustlers, solopreneurs, creators are unable to give 1-2 hours daily in book reading. Here 'tiwonk' stands out. We extract the messages from the books and deliver it to the reader. 'A Book, A Day' - is our mission. 'tiwonk' works to help readers to grow, to improve, to upgrade in their lifes.
Is it free ?
Yes, tiwonk is absolutely free to use. No charge will be taken from readers to read any book or to use any feature. While the platfrom contains ads. If the reader wants a ad-free service, he/she can upgrade to premium version with a subcribtion plan.
